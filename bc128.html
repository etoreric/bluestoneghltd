Automated Blood Culture System

BC128

Overview

Innovative system provides non-invasive automated continuous monitoring for every individual bottle.

Simple operation and dual barcode management system for random access or preset entry of bottle.

Expandable modular design, standard configuration with one module which accommodates 128 test bottles, can be extended up to 256 test bottles, model BC64,BC128,BC256 are available.

Efficient and Reliable

Detect microbial growth of blood specimens or sterile body fluids.
Continuous agitation and incubation of the blood culture bottles, improve rapid detection and discovery rate of the most fastidious bacteria;
Positive report is given within 4-24 hours for the majority of microorganisms.
Independent detection sensor for every single bottle, bottles are tested every 10 minutes.
Advanced algorithm for individual bottle type ensures reliable performance in special circumstances such as low blood volume, pediatric specimens or detection of microorganisms with low growth and Neisseria.

Easy and user-oriented

All procedures are easy and simple for “scan barcode” to “load vials”, maximumly save time and simply the workflow.
Display of real-time growth curve.
Designed with colorimetric technology, compatible with other instrument /media based on the same technology.
