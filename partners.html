Our Partners

At Blue Stone Limited, we believe that collaboration is the key to advancing healthcare and delivering exceptional solutions.
Our trusted partners play a pivotal role in enabling us to provide cutting-edge medical products, services, and technologies that improve patient outcomes worldwide.

RENDER BIOTECH CO., LTD

RENDER BIOTECH CO.,LTD(Render) is established in April of 2016, is a innovative
company that specialized in developing, manufacturing and distributing in-vitro diagnostic
instrument and reagents in Thromboelastography, POCT Immunoassay and Microbiological.
Render is headquarters in Guangming, Shenzhen with GMP and ISO 13485 certified manufacturing
facilities in Asia and distributors around the globe.

ARI Medical

ARI Medical, a leading provider of medical equipment and solutions in China,
brings to the table more than two decades of expertise in delivering comprehensive medical
equipment solutions. ARI unifies the manufacture, research, development, and distribution
of medical equipment within a singular entity comprising three factories. Our headquarters
is situated in Hefei City, Anhui province. and our production bases are located in the major
medical industry hubs of Anhui, Jiangsu, and Guangdong provinces.

Axiom Laboratories

Axiom Laboratories is aimed at providing high quality products at the reasonable
prices with focus on customer service for over 20 years. Our customers include universities
& research Institutions, hospitals, microbiology, biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.
